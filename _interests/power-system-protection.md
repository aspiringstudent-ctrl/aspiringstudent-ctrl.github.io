My research interests focus on the development of intelligent and resilient power system protection schemes for modern electric grids. As power systems become increasingly complex due to the integration of renewable energy resources, distributed generation, power electronics, and digital communication technologies, conventional protection methods face new challenges in maintaining system reliability and security.

By combining my practical experience in power distribution systems with graduate training in statistics and machine learning, I seek to contribute to the design of intelligent, adaptive, and data-driven protection systems that improve the reliability, security, and resilience of future electric power networks.
