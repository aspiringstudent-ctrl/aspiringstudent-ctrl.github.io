I am interested in developing intelligent and resilient smart grid technologies that enhance the reliability, efficiency, and flexibility of modern electric power systems. My research focuses on integrating advanced sensing, communication, and data analytics to improve system monitoring, control, and decision-making. I am particularly interested in leveraging real-time operational data to support adaptive grid management, fault resilience, and the seamless integration of distributed energy resources within increasingly decentralized power networks.
